The bone. 138 members → 552 curved sub-beams · 11.04 g · hollow tubes (0.8 mm wall) · every surface ≥ 1.5 mm · no spikes, no loose ends, one piece · 498 µm at the wells against a 500 µm gate · 254 pillars, 0 props.

The sensor. Each well is a contactless-Hall flexure — a magnet on a printed TPU dome over a 3-axis Hall, soft in tilt and in plunge, snapping for a click. Scored against the muscle-effort model across the 5th–95th percentile hand: every well is five-way on every finger — the ulnar "three-way" limit was a cradle artefact, not muscle.

The strap. The band is the convex hull of (skin ∪ device), so it rides over the gauntlet it holds down; a watch-lug captures a pin in shear at each anchor foot; one adjustable strap (21 mm range) fits the 5th–95th percentile population.
